# BankerWiz 🎩✨

**AI-Powered Credit Underwriting for Community Banks**

BankerWiz helps community bankers produce credit underwriting memos, create problem-solving narratives, and generate consistent documentation — all in minutes, not hours.

## What is BankerWiz?

BankerWiz is an AI product built specifically for community banks. It understands banking terminology, regulatory requirements, and lending workflows out of the box.

### Key Features

- **Credit Underwriting Memos** — Generate comprehensive credit analysis memos with risk assessments, financial summaries, and recommendations
- **Problem-Solving Narratives** — Craft clear, compelling narratives that explain complex credit situations and support sound lending decisions
- **Consistent Memo Standards** — Every memo follows your bank's exact template and formatting, regardless of which loan officer creates it
- **Faster Processing** — Cut memo turnaround from days to minutes for quicker approvals and more loans closed
- **Trust & Org Document Review** — AI-powered analysis of trust documents, articles of incorporation, bylaws, and operating agreements
- **Audit & Compliance Ready** — Structured outputs with clean audit trails that examiners appreciate

## How It Works

1. **Upload Your Data** — Drop in financial statements, borrower info, or existing documents
2. **AI Analyzes & Writes** — BankerWiz applies banking standards, calculates ratios, and drafts a complete memo
3. **Review & Finalize** — Review the AI draft, make edits, and submit. You stay in control
